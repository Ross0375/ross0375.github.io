Procreate

After spending months trying to learn to use a drawing tablet on the computer, I decided to bite the bullet and invest in an Ipad for digital illustrations, and it has helped me in numerous ways. A lot of my procreate work is heavily inspired by the psychedelic 60's style of art with a tattoo style stippling/shading that I enjoy making.
